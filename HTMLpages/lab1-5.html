Almond Green Salad

Ingredients

1/2 pound bacon
2 tablespoons white wine vingar
3 tablespoons honey
1/2 teaspoon dry hot mustard
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon ground paprike
1/4 cup olive oil
1 head red leaf lettuce torn into bite size pieces
1 (15 ounce) can of mandaarin oranges, drained
1 bunch green onions, diced
3/4 cup slivered almonds

Directions

1. In medium skillet over medium-heat, cook bacon until evenly browned, drain cool, and crumble.
2. To make the dressing, throughly blend the vinegar, honey, dry mustard, celery salt, paprika, and olive oil.
3. place lettuce, oranges, green onions, bacon, and almonds in serving bowl. Toss with dressing and serve.
